ingrediants required

  • oreo biscuit
  • baking soda
  • milk
  • all purpose floar
  • ghee
  • sugar
  • choclate esscence(optional)
  • butter

steps in making oreo cake

  1. take all the ingrediants listed above
  2. cut the all oreo biscuit into small peaces,grind them and make thin powder of biscuit
  3. transfer biscuit mixture to a bowl and add some milk to it and make a thin dough
  4. add a table spoon of baking soda to mixture and keep the mixture for rest in one hour
  5. transfer the mixture to the cookware which is ghee greased
  6. place the cookware on low flame for around an half hour
  7. off the flame and remove the cake from cookware and your cake is ready
